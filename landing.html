Welcome to Rate Music! (Better Name Incoming)

To get started with this service, you can click on albums or reviews in the navigation bar to view some albums and reviews.

If you would like to post your own review, click Rate Music in the navbar to view the registration page. Once you register, you will be automatically logged in and taken to the albums page. You can search for albums here and select an album to see its reviews.

As a returning user, you can select login to get back to posting reviews.
